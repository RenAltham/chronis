THE WATCH PREDATES THE CHRONIS
Watson's pocket watch doesn't come from The Chronis. It predates it. If The Chronis controls all of reality but can't read the watch, then the watch operates on rules from before reality had rules. What if the watch is from whatever existed before The Convergence? Before the Avatars?

The residual frequency at 396 Hz isn't just an echo — it's construction noise. Sherry isn't dead. She's been disassembled across all timelines and she's using the energy of every Amelia who remembers her to build a new timeline from scratch.
